## Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant.

### Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

### Our Standards

- Be respectful and considerate.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community.

### Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported via GitHub issues or directly to the maintainers.
